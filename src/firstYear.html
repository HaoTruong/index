    First Year Second Year Something else here

This is a small drawing I did while having a Calculus II lecture in spring semester of 2019, it is somehow representing me as a first-year student coming to University of Cincinnati, running away from deadlines I would call it. I was overwhelmed with amount of homework, project that I need to be done in the end of every semester, and studying as less as I used to do in high school did not help; Studying alone also did not help. Waking up early and staying up late for studying seems to be something normal to a college student which I do not like. However, beside from being stress out from studying, being in lectures, working on a project, first-year experience was a great year after all. It shaped me into a better version of myself that I completely love it and way better than a goal I put out in the beginning of the year. I used to keep everything to myself and sharing it out was a big deal to me, but I met friends who I can share, talk and study with. I learned a lot of new things from first year, social issues I learned from Honor Gateway have the most value to me for the whole year including all classes that I took. I grown up more than I expected, I start to think about everything happens around me more than just myself, and it motivates me to get out there and solve others problem more than just my problem; I want to work for something that is bigger than myself.
